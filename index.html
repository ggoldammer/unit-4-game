Crystals Collector

Instructions:

  • You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • Click the four crystals below to add to your total score.
  • You win by matching your score with your given number.
  • You lose by going over your given number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • Each time the game restarts, the value of the gems will be restarted.
